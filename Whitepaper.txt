Abstract
SFOX is a next-generation digital asset designed to combine scarcity, network growth, and user
participation into a unified mining ecosystem. The protocol introduces a time-based emission
model, a structured participation system, and a capped supply to ensure long-term
sustainability. By aligning incentives between early adopters, active contributors, and network
builders, SFOX creates a self-sustaining digital economy powered by engagement rather than
capital.

8. Mining Formula
Total mining rate is calculated as:
● Mining rate = Base rate × (Rank + Pioneer + sum of referral bonuses)
Where:
● Base rate = current global mining rate (e.g., 0.25 SFOX/h after halving)
● Rank multiplier = base multiplier for your rank:
○ Pioneer = 1.5x
○ Lord = 5x
○ Baron = 15x
○ President = 30x
○ Titan = 50x
● Pioneer permanent bonus = +1.5x additive (only if user earned Pioneer in first 30 days,
applies to Lord+ ranks)
● Total active referral bonus = (number of active referrals) × (bonus % for your rank)
8
Example (Titan with Pioneer bonus, 10 active referrals)
● Base rate = 0.25 SFOX/h
● Titan rank multiplier = 50x
● Pioneer permanent bonus = +1.5x → total rank multiplier = 51.5x
● Active referrals = 10 → each gives +25% → total bonus = 10 × 25% = 250% (2.5x)
● Final mining rate = 0.25 × ( 51.5 + (10 × 25%) ) = 13.5 SFOX/h
This creates a system where:
● Growth = higher rewards
● Activity = sustained rewards
● Inactivity = zero contribution
